League of Legend game of MOBA genre being developed and serviced by Riot Games. It is called LoL in Korea and League in the West after the game name, and League in the West. It gained high popularity by lowering entry barriers compared to previous MOBA (AOS) games, and now has many users all over the world. It is one of the most popular PC games in the world.

Numerous e-sports competitions are being held, including the League of Legend World Championship, which has the largest number of viewers among the world's E-sports competitions, and regional leagues. It was also adopted as an official exhibition event at the 2018 Asian Games in Jakarta and Palembang.
